The loop

1
Burn to buy. Spend $CHRONIC on seeds & gear. 60% is burned forever, 40% drops into this week's pool.
2
Grow, water & sell. Plant a seed, tap it to water (each splash speeds growth), then sell the ripe buds. Every sale earns XP — fancier strains pay more, and each harvest rolls a quality: 🔥 Fire or 💎 Exotic multiply your XP.
3
Climb. XP ranks you on the weekly leaderboard. Burn more, grow more, climb higher.
4
Top 3 win. When the 7-day season ends, the top 3 growers split the whole pool proportional to their XP. Then it resets and the race starts again.
No dev cut — 100% of every spend goes to the burn or the players' pool. Prize is paid in $CHRONIC.

Live mode uses your Solana wallet: each buy is one transaction that burns 60% and sends 40% to the pool, verified on-chain. Demo mode simulates it locally so you can feel the loop first.
