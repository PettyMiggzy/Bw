The loop

1
Burn to plant. Spend $CHRONIC on seeds & gear. 60% is burned forever, 40% drops into this week's pool.
2
Grow & sell. Plants ripen, you sell the buds. Every sale earns XP — fancier strains pay more.
3
Climb. XP ranks you on the weekly leaderboard. Burn more, grow more, climb higher.
4
Top 3 win. When the 7-day season ends, the top 3 growers split the whole pool proportional to their XP. Then it resets and the race starts again.
No dev cut — 100% of every spend goes to the burn or the players' pool. Prize is paid in $CHRONIC.

This is a front-end preview: balances, pool and leaderboard are simulated locally and reset on refresh of the season. Wallet + on-chain settlement come next.
