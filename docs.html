Supply & burns — how it's locked

$CHRONIC supply is permanently capped and only goes down. Two on-chain guarantees, both verifiable on any Solana explorer:

  • Mint authority renounced — the 1,000,000,000 max supply can never increase. No one can mint new tokens, ever.
  • Freeze authority renounced — no wallet can be frozen or blacklisted.
  • Deflationary by SPL Burn — $CHRONIC is removed from circulation via on-chain SPL Burn, which destroys tokens outright (they're subtracted from total supply, not parked in a dead wallet). So the live total supply on-chain is always below 1B and falling.

Because burns destroy rather than store, no "dead address" holds the burned tokens — they cease to exist. The shrinking total supply is the proof, readable directly from the mint.
