📚 how do you even get rugged on solana? (read this)
  • here's the thing nobody tells you: on pump.fun / bonk, every coin is the exact same standard token program. there's no custom contract to "read." so the rug isn't in the code — it's in these five facts on-chain:
  • 🔥 mint authority — if it's still ACTIVE, the dev can print infinite new supply and dump it on you. should be revoked.
  • 🧊 freeze authority — if ACTIVE, the dev can freeze YOUR wallet so you can't sell while they exit. should be revoked. hardest red flag.
  • 🐋 top wallet — if one hand holds a big % of supply, they can nuke the chart in a single sell.
  • 👥 top 10 / bundle — if ten wallets (often one person's bundle) hold most of it, they can coordinate the exit.
  • 💧 liquidity — on the bonding curve it's escrowed and can't be pulled. once it migrates to an AMM, the LP must be burned/locked or the dev can yank it.
  • learn these five and you'll dodge most rugs without reading a line of code. that's the whole game. 🦴
